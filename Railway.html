Features

1. Locate Facilities

• Interactive Maps: The platform offers detailed, interactive maps of the station, showing the exact location of facilities like restrooms, ticket counters, platforms, waiting areas, food courts, and more.
• Facility Categories: Users can filter and search for specific types of facilities (e.g., accessible restrooms, baby care rooms, or vegetarian food options) based on their needs.
• Proximity Indicators: The system can indicate how far you are from a selected facility, helping you to decide the most efficient route.



2. Real-Time Navigation

• Turn-by-Turn Directions: The platform offers clear, concise directions that update in real-time as you move through the station.
• Connection Alerts: If you’re transferring between trains, the system can     alert you to changes in your connection, such as platform shifts or delays, and guide you.
• Congestion Avoidance: The navigation system can suggest alternative     routes within the station to avoid congested areas, helping you save time and reduce stress.



3. Accessibility Support

• Accessible Routes: The system highlights paths that are wheelchair-friendly, with elevators, ramps, and accessible restrooms marked clearly.
• Voice Guidance: For visually impaired users, the platform offers voice-guided navigation to help them move through the station safely and efficiently.



4. Multi-Language Support

• Multilingual Interface: Upon accessing the platform, users are prompted to select their preferred language from a list of available options. The platform then adjusts its interface, displaying all menus, instructions, and information in the chosen language.
• Language Customization: The system allows users to change their language preference at any time during their journey. This flexibility is useful for bilingual travelers or in cases where users need to switch between languages for any reason.



5. Seamless Integration

• Integrate our navigation system with existing railway apps for a smooth, unified travel experience.
• Ticket Integration: The navigation system can integrate with your digital train tickets, automatically setting up navigation from your arrival point to your departure platform.
• Real-Time Updates: The system pulls real-time data from the railway's service updates, offering you the latest information on train schedules, platform changes, and delays.
• Cross-App Functionality: If you are using multiple railway-related apps (like ticketing, loyalty programs, or customer service), this system can work alongside them, providing a seamless transition between apps without the need to input your data multiple times.
